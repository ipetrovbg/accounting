Accounts

This accounting system, allows you to create accounts as many as you need.

The process of creating and editing an account is made very simple.

You can choose the name and the currency, so it will reflect your real accounts!

Transactions

Creat transactions for all your purchases, bills, incomes etc.

When you create a transaction, you can change the note or the date of when it was made.

The dates and categories are very handy. Later you can query based on these criteria.

Filter and Search

Filtering is very powerful tool! Especially when you have tens of thousands transactions per month.

With our filters you can query transactions only for today or tomorrow. Transactions in specific category or a phrase in notes.

You can see all of your transactions that are more than certain amount or in between a range!

Graphs

Often we are too busy or just can't see how much money we are spending in some areas.

For that purpose we are creating dynamic graphs on every transaction that you make, so you can have better understanding where exactly your money goes!

Transfer Money

You can easily transfer your money from one account to other!

If the accounts are in different currencies the system will automatically convert the money based on the current rate.

But if you are not agree with the online rate, you have the freedom to choose your own for every transaction!

Money Per Day

This is an unique feature.

You can see how much money you can spend per day, based on the money in your current selected account.

This is very important if you are in the habit of spending money without counting. Like me!

It really helps, when you have something to remind you about, how much you can afford, without getting in troubles!
